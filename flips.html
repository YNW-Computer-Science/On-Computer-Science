The Flips Project

The Flips Project is a program that began with a mission to make the joy of Gymnastics accessible to financially challenged Families in Central Oregon. Boys and girls ages 6-10 can bounce around during a class one hour a week for an 8 week session at OOA Gymnastics. The Class is taught by high school volunteers that have been involved in gymnastics themselves. Kids will get to learn balance, agility, and body awareness as well as fun tricks on Bault, Bars, Beam, Floor, and Tramp. Come join us for some fun, movement education, and beginning gymnastics!
